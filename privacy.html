Privacy Policy

DeskBreak respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

DeskBreak does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device.

Notifications

DeskBreak uses local notifications to remind you to take breaks. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent to any server.

Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, authentication is handled securely by Apple. DeskBreak does not access or store your personal information beyond what is required for login.

Data Storage

Your settings (work schedule, timers, preferences) are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your data to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

DeskBreak does not use ads, analytics tracking, or third-party data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

DeskBreak does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
