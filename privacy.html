Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

When you create or sign in to an account, we may collect:

  • First name

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

Your account information is securely stored using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service provided by Google. Firebase applies industry-standard security and encryption measures to protect your data.

Notifications

DeskBreak uses local notifications to remind you to take breaks. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

DeskBreak uses trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase (Google) – database and authentication
  • Sign in with Apple – secure login

These services may process your information according to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
