Privacy Policy

DeskBreak respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

When you sign in and use the app, we may collect:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Email address
  • Firebase user ID (account identifier)
  • App usage events (for product analytics)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Identify you within the app
  • Store and manage your account record
  • Understand feature usage and improve the app experience
  • Provide customer support

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data for advertising or marketing.

Data Storage

Your account information is stored using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. We also use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage. Firebase applies industry-standard security and encryption measures to protect your data.

Analytics and Identifiers

DeskBreak uses Firebase Analytics to collect usage events (for example, sign-in, settings changes, and account actions). These analytics events may be associated with your Firebase account identifier so we can understand usage patterns and troubleshoot account-specific issues.

We do not use your data for third-party advertising and we do not perform cross-app tracking.

Notifications

DeskBreak uses local notifications to remind you to take breaks. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

DeskBreak uses trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase (Google) – authentication, database, and analytics
  • Sign in with Apple – secure login

These services may process your information according to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children’s Privacy

DeskBreak does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active. You can request account deletion from within the app settings. When deleted, we remove your user record from our Firestore database and clear local account data from your device.
